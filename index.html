About Me

I'm a Software Engineer at Zoho with 4+ years of experience building software that people actually use. I started in Android, working with Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, and modern app architecture. That background still shapes how I think about reliability, performance, and user experience.

Today my work spans backend services, REST APIs, and product engineering on Zoho Notebook. I'm deepening my skills in Java, Jersey, and server-side systems while continuing to contribute across mobile and web. The stack has grown. The goal is still the same: build things that solve real problems.

I try to think like a product engineer. I care about what users need, the details that matter, and working well with design and product teams. I use AI tools in my workflow to move faster and explore ideas, but good fundamentals and clear design still matter most.

I'm interested in scalable systems, engineering productivity, and building software that holds up over time. Backend service, mobile screen, or web page: I want the work to be clear, maintainable, and useful.
